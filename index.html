Our Story

SentimentAI was founded by four entrepreneurs who shared a vision: to make advanced NLP technology accessible to everyone. Frustrated by complex and expensive sentiment analysis tools, Mehak, Chris, Alex, and Tyler combined their expertise to build a platform that's both powerful and easy to use.

Today, SentimentAI serves thousands of businesses and developers, providing fast, accurate sentiment analysis through our cloud-based platform. From startups to enterprises, we're transforming how organizations understand customer feedback and text data.
